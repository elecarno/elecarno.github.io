The Voidquill Archive

Founded by Astoria, 31st Daughter of the Voidquills of the Four Pillars Federation, the Voidquill Archive is an ongoing effort to catalogue the experiences of humanity in post-Symbiote era of our history. This includes both recovered writings from the age of the Federation, as well as newer materials such as from the Avalon or more recently Eden systems.
