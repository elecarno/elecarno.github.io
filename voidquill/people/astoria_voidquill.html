Astoria Voidquill

Astoria, 31st Daughter of the Voidquills of the Four Pillars Federation. One of the last survivors of her Federal House, Astoria signed up for the Sunnyhome expedition in 2220, crash landing in Avalon with the other colonists aboard the ship shortly after the political collapse of the FPF.

Almost immediately upon landing in Avalon, Astoria began writing her poetry - joining the Keystone Corporation to survive in the unknown system. Early in 2221 however, she disappeared into deep space, re-emerging in the Eden system in a KCO lifeboat with no memory as to how she arrived there.

Joining forces with the Tempest Wolf Corporation, the successor to the FPF founding faction of the same name, Astoria now works to catalogue the experiences of humanity in the post-Symbiote universe through her work with the Voidquill Archive.
