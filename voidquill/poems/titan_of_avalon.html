Titan of Avalon

by Astoria Voidquill

The quiet titan of Avalon,
ever watchful
above or below
left or right,
always,
in the corners of my helmet,
rings piercing the screen
that even sunlight
will not breach.

Swirling are its clouds,
spiral is its draw.
Ever closer the bands do pull,
upon my thrusters set to full.

The quiet titan of Avalon,
ever in my mind.
For weary sailors flying blind,
hoping it is home they'll find.

2221.02.02

About

The final piece written by Astoria Voidquill before her disapperance from the Avalon system. It tells of the looming prescence of the great ringed gas giant within the system and the psychological effects such a colossal celestial body had on a space-born such as herself.
