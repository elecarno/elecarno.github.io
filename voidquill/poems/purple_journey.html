Purple Journey

by Arkady Orlunda

Lydian, Lydian who fought in the
deep blue heavens, who braved the
silver snow, who soared the skies of
pink smog and searched for days for the
compatriots of new lands, new spaces soon to be
gold and white and gold glistening from through the
smoke and fog to pierce the great veil of war and serve
justice. Justice to an unfeeling but all-seeing that no sooner
wept for its losses as it did tear at worlds.

Lydian, Lydian who brought me home from between
blue & white, who gave me warmth and taught me the vision of the
Four Pillars,
rest now, for the Purple Journey has come to an end,
and in that seventh star's place you will forever shine a
beacon of our young
Federation.

c. 2200
