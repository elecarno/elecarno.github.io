The Seventh Star

by Dahlia Voidquill

Window, a gaze back on our escape,
I carry the torch, a flame of the Seventh Star,
entrusted by Orlunda — given memory of a planet.
Golden embers that shall bridge the great expanse,
manifest ideals, once more a structure to the stars.

And yet, the great violet burst stains the cosmos,
our vanquished enemy reaching with tendrils
forever into the abyss we call home.

But no matter — as those before us,
our fire will kindle and torch the beast,
bring forth old homes, and relight the lost systems.

And I, entrusted vestige of planets now broken,
carry our words unto the starry sails of our future,
unforgotten in the mist of the Seventh Star.

c. 2200

About

"A Recovered poem written by Dahlia Voidquill (otherwise recorded using her full title: Dahlia, 1st Daughter of the Voidquills of the Four Pillars Federation). Dahlia was the first in the Voidquill lineage, and this poem suggests that it was Arkady Orlunda herself that appointed Dahlia this role, however this of course it only speculation. The poem is believed to have been written sometime during the evacuation of ATK-07 - the destroyed system being referred to in this poem and other pieces of FPF-era literature as the "Seventh Star".

Though she is a well known figure amongst the Voidquills (I myself being the 31st Daughter of the Voidquills), much of Dahlia's pre-Voidqull life remains a mystery. Myself and the team at the Voidquill Archive have decided to make it a priority to recover any and all information regarding Dahlia Voidquill, and we will of course be publicly updating the archive with our findings as they occur."

— Voidquill Archive entry 2222.07.27 written by Astoria Voidquill
