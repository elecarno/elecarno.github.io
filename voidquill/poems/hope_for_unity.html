Hope for Unity

by Astoria Voidquill

Let down thy weapons,
let down thy shields.
Cold space is death enough,
the grave needs not more bodies.
So take up tools, not arms,
and forge the weld strongest of all.
The greatest pact of true strength,
the bond - of Hope for Unity.

2222.08.17
